About

I've spent 20 years in technology, moving from the engine room — Linux systems, virtualisation, infrastructure — to the boardroom conversation about what technology actually costs and whether it's worth it.

That journey gave me something rare: I speak both languages. I understand how engineers think about architecture decisions, and I understand how CFOs think about returns. FinOps is precisely that intersection.

At AVIV Group, I lead cloud financial management across Germany, Belgium, and France — optimising spend, building governance frameworks, and making the case to C-level that cloud economics is a strategic lever, not a line item to minimise.

My goal is to grow into a VP role where I can build and lead organisations that institutionalise this discipline at scale.

Philosophy

"Cloud spend isn't a cost to minimise. It's an investment to govern — and that's a very different conversation."

Bridging languages

Technology and Finance speak different dialects. The most valuable thing I do is translate — so both sides can make better decisions together.

Governance over restriction

The goal isn't to stop teams from spending. It's to ensure every euro of cloud investment has accountability, visibility, and a clear business rationale.

Pragmatic over perfect

Frameworks that live in spreadsheets don't change behaviour. I build systems that people actually use — because they make their work easier, not harder.
